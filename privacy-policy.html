Privacy Policy for SmartPinManager

Effective Date: January 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SmartPinManager ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application uses your data when you connect it with the Pinterest API.

2. Data We Collect

Our application is designed for internal content management and strictly operates with the data you explicitly authorize via Pinterest OAuth.

  • Pinterest Account Information: We access your public profile information (username, profile image) to verify account ownership.
  • Content Data: We access your Boards and Pins data to enable scheduling and content publication features.
  • Images: Images uploaded through the application are temporarily processed to be sent to Pinterest servers and are not permanently stored on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the accessed data solely for the purpose of:

  • Publishing Pins to your specified boards.
  • Retrieving comments to assist in community management.
  • Creating new boards as requested by the user.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

4. Data Storage

SmartPinManager operates as a local/client-side tool. Your Pinterest Access Tokens are stored securely within your local environment configuration and are never transmitted to external analytics servers.
